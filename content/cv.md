*The nonsense-mediated mRNA decay pathway modulates Sonic hedgehog signaling and medulloblastoma aggressiveness*

- Developed and optimised cell-based functional assay platforms including proliferation assays (Ki67, BrdU, MTS, Alamar Blue), dose-response assays (EC50/IC50), and transcriptional and translational reporter assays to characterise how NMD activity relates to Shh pathway signalling in this system
